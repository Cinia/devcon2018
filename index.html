{ Theday outside thebox }

Cinia DEVcon 2017 is a meeting for all the employees of Cinia Solutions. It is meant to be a more technical get together where we look at the world outside. Cinia

We have three internal speakers who will speak about technologies and experiences outside their normal day to day routine at work. We also have an external keynote speaker who will tell us about the world of Blockchain.

Workshops in DEVcon are not your normal paper exercises. We have gallery of your work in the waiting area. We have Full Stack game in one room. Have you tested virtual reality yet? If not, now you have a chance for that too.

And after a hard days work it always nice to relax and what a better way to do that than have some good food, drinks and hot sauna.
